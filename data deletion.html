Data Deletion Policy - BETTING PREDICTION

Last Updated: January 14, 2025

Your Right to Data Deletion

At BETTING PREDICTION, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, authentication data
  • Profile Data: User preferences and settings
  • Usage History: App interaction logs and session data
  • Personal Identifiers: Device identifiers linked to your account
  • Communication Records: Support tickets and correspondence

How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the BETTING PREDICTION app
  2. Go to Settings or Profile section
  3. Select "Delete Account" or "Data Deletion"
  4. Confirm your request

Method 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to: ibrahimolasunkanmi468@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Data Deletion Request - BETTING PREDICTION"
  3. Include your registered email address
  4. State your request clearly: "I request deletion of my account and all personal data"

Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Account access is disabled
  • Within 7 days: Personal data removed from active systems
  • Within 30 days: Complete deletion from all systems and backups

Important Notes

  • Irreversible Process: Data deletion cannot be undone
  • New Account Required: You'll need to create a new account to use the app again
  • Legal Retention: Some data may be retained if required by law

Data We Cannot Delete

Certain information may be retained for legal or security purposes:

  • Transaction records (if required by law)
  • Fraud prevention data
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data
  • Data required for ongoing legal proceedings

Third-Party Data

Data collected by third-party services (Google AdMob, Google Sign-In) is governed by their respective privacy policies. You may need to contact them directly for deletion:

  • Google: Google Account Data & Privacy
  • AdMob: Ads Settings

Verification Process

To protect your privacy, we may require verification of your identity before processing deletion requests. This may include:

  • Confirming your registered email address
  • Answering security questions
  • Providing additional verification if requested

Confirmation

Once your data deletion is complete, we will send a confirmation email to your registered address. If you don't receive confirmation within 30 days, please contact us.
